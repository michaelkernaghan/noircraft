The Fellowship Code

Our shadow fellowship thrives on mutual respect, shared knowledge, and the pursuit of privacy excellence. Follow these sacred tenets to maintain harmony in our realm.

Respect & Honor

Treat all guild members with respect regardless of their mastery level. We were all apprentices once, and knowledge grows through patience and understanding.

Share Knowledge Freely

The shadow arts grow stronger when knowledge is shared. Help fellow members, answer questions, and contribute to the collective wisdom of our fellowship.

Privacy First

Champion privacy in all discussions. Our mission is to advance privacy-preserving technologies and protect individual rights across all realms.

Innovation & Excellence

Strive for excellence in all shadow contract creations. Push the boundaries of what's possible while maintaining the highest standards of quality.
